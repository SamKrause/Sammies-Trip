Places in California

Disneyland

Sammie went to Disneyland and met Micky and Minnie Mouse for the first time. She loved all of the characters and the rides. She especially loved the parade! After all of the fun, he fell asleep during the firework show.

Paramount Pictures Studio

Sammie's uncle Dan works for Paramount Pictures, so she came to see his "office". She turned a lot of heads while she was there because everyone thought she was a movie star.

The California Beach

Even though it was January, Sammie played on the beach. She had a nice evening stroll in Malibu and then some fun in the sun in Santa Monica.
